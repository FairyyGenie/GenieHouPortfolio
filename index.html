Artists Go

A fullstack MERN app

Created by Genie Hou, Derek Han, Isabel Chen, and Joseph Yusufov.
ArtistsGo allows college students to exchange artistic products and services and also inspire collaboration.
Through ArtistsGo, fellow students can buy another student's handmade pottery, beaded necklaces, or even dance lessons.
It is a platform for student artists to market their products and create a customer base.
The app is developed with the full MERN stack, with MongoDB, NodeJS, Express, and React.
ArtistsGo is created for students to share and connect.

Music and Order

Processing game

This is a game designed to test the players ability to match frequencies coming from the arduino panel with a note.
The black and white circle represents the questions and when being pressed the arduino panel will make a sound matching the frequency of a note.
The user needs to pull the C, D, E, F, G, A, B choices to match with circles.
The LED lights on the panel will tell the users which ones are right and which ones are wrong.

A Happy Place

Unity based VR experience

This is a project designed for people to share their happy-places.
The VR experience consists of three scenes which are my personal happy places.
First is the beach scene, second is the Snow mountain scene, and last is the view in space scene.
In each of the scene, there is a door for the users to find and reach the next destination.
The landing scene consists of a gallery of the pictures I took of the travel destination I have been to and three doors leading the users to the three scenes.
Through the experience, I aim to share with the audience the happy places in my mind.
